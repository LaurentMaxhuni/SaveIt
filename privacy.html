SaveIt Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 1, 2026

Summary

SaveIt is built to be private by design. We can only get website content and absolutely nothing else, and nothing is saved by the extension or any server.

What we access

  • The content of the single webpage you choose to save.

What we do not access

  • Passwords, form entries, cookies, or authentication tokens.
  • Browsing history or other tabs.
  • Device files, contacts, or any system data.

Data storage and sharing

No data is stored, retained, or transmitted. The extension only triggers a download to your device, and does not keep a copy of the content anywhere.
