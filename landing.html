Privacy Policy

SaveIt only accesses the website content you choose to save. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • We only read the content of the current website page you trigger.
  • We do not access passwords, cookies, browsing history, or other tabs.
  • No data is stored or retained by the extension or any server.
